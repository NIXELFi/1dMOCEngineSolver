**Fuel Type:** 100 (octane)

**Stroke (mm):** 42.5

## Induction

- **Type:** Naturally Aspirated through 20 mm Restrictor
- **Throttle Body:** Bosch 32 mm ETC, butterfly valve, max flow 14.477 lb/min, variable pedal-to-TPS position maps
- **Fuel Injection:** Deatschwerks 300cc FSAE Injector, conical spray, up directed at intake valve
- **Fuel Pressure:** 4 bar, static
- **Injector Location:** 155.5 mm before intake valve, directed downwards towards back of intake valve

### Fuel System Sensors
Crank/Cam Position, Throttle Position, Manifold Pressure, Intake Temperature,
Coolant Temperature, Fuel Pressure, Oil Temperature, Exhaust Gas Temperature,
Ambient Pressure

## Intake

- **Plenum Volume (cc):** 1500
- **Runner Length (mm):** 245.0

## Exhaust

- **Header Configuration:** 4-2-1
- **Effective Runner Lengths (mm):** 308, 392
- **Variation (mm):** 3, 5
- **Primary Diameter (mm):** 31.8
- **Secondary Diameter (mm):** 38.1
- **Collector Diameters (mm):** 38.1, 50.8

## Ignition

- **ECU:** Link G4X FuryX
- **Igniter Module:** Bosch 0-227-100-211
- **Coils:** CBR600RR OEM
- **Spark Plugs:** NGK IMR9C-9HES
- **Timing Strategy:** MAP vs RPM, 15 deg base, 40 deg max advance

## Oiling

- **Type:** 2.4 L Wet Sump
- **Oil Pan:** Custom aluminum billet, lateral baffle flaps, centralized PPA-CF pickup
- **Oil:** Motul 300V 10w-30

## Cooling

- **Radiator:** Centrally mounted 2 core single pass
- **Fan:** 561 cfm @ 10 mm H2O static pressure, full shroud

## Fuel Tank

- **Type:** Aluminum, mounted behind firewall and driver seat, forward of main roll hoop
- **Features:** Vertical baffle, custom intake regulator and filter
- **Capacity (L):** 6.1

## Muffler

Ticon 2.5" Titanium Race Muffler, 17" OAL
